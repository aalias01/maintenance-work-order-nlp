What Makes This Different

🏭

Domain Data

Corpus generated with real maintenance vocabulary from 12 years of industrial engineering — HVAC, subsea, manufacturing. Realistic abbreviations, failure taxonomies, and work-order style.

⚡

LoRA Fine-Tuning

DistilBERT fine-tuned with LoRA adapters: <1% of parameters updated, near-identical F1 to full fine-tune, ~3× faster training. Adapter weights in megabytes, not gigabytes.

🤖

LLM ETL Extraction

GPT-4o-mini with Pydantic structured output lifts root-cause F1 from ~65% to ~90%. A hybrid extractor runs regex first and escalates only low-confidence records to the LLM — 97% of the accuracy at 20% of the API cost.
